Privacy Protocol v2.1

> 01. The Local Vault Architecture

SortingSource operates strictly as a local-first software engine. We do not harbor, transmit, or observe your generated leads, email copy, or reply logs on centralized servers. All data is written exclusively to your local hardware via the outbound_crm.db and localStorage.

> 02. API Key Transmission

Your integrated API keys (Google, OpenAI, Hugging Face, Twilio) and SMTP/IMAP credentials are encrypted within your Local Vault. When the engine executes, these keys communicate directly with their respective third-party providers. SortingSource master servers never see, touch, or log your private credentials.
